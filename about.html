Our Story

Founded in 2026, VELORA was born from a simple idea: fashion should be timeless, accessible, and empowering. We believe that what you wear is an extension of who you are, which is why we meticulously curate collections that blend classic silhouettes with modern sensibilities.

Our journey started in a small studio in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Today, we serve thousands of customers nationwide, bringing premium quality apparel right to their doorsteps. We are committed to sustainable practices, ethical sourcing, and providing an unparalleled shopping experience.
